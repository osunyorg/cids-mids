We are excited to announce that the applications for the MIDS 2025-2026 are now open! Complete applications must be submitted by 15 December 2024.

It is this time of year again: applications for the MIDS 2025-2026 are now open!

This is a unique opportunity for aspiring international dispute settlement professionals to join a dynamic, innovative, and rigorous program designed to equip them with the advanced knowledge and skills needed for successful and fulfilling careers. With a rigorous curriculum, specialized training, and comprehensive career support, the MIDS offers a pathway to excel in a rapidly evolving global landscape.

Applications are open until 15 December 2024, 23:59 (CET). We encourage all interested candidates to start working on their application as early and possible and take the first step toward what could be a transformative educational, professional, and personal experience with us in Geneva.
