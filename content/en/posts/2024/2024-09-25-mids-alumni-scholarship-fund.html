The MIDS is launching a call for donations to go towards the annual MIDS Alumni Scholarship.

contents_reading_time: seconds: 20 text: >- less than a minute contents: - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

The MIDS Alumni Scholarship Fund, established in September 2018, provides financial support to one MIDS student each academic year. Every contribution, no matter the size, goes directly to the student, empowering them to pursue their educational dreams. Your generosity ensures that financial constraints do not hinder their journey with us.

If you wish to contribute to the MIDS Alumni Scholarship Fund, please visit the MIDS Alumni page for more information on how to make a donation.

Thank you for your generosity!
