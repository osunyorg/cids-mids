Academic excellence in the heart of Geneva
The MIDS is taught by some of the most respected voices in international
dispute settlement, including:


Professor Zachary Douglas KC - Leading authority on investment arbitration
Professor Gabrielle Kaufmann-Kohler - Former President of the Swiss Arbitration Association

Alexander Fessas - Secretary General of the ICC International Court of Arbitration

Students benefit from direct engagement with faculty who shape global
legal practice - through lectures, tutorials, and seminars.

 

Located in Geneva, the MIDS offers unmatched proximity to institutions
like:


The World Trade
Organization (WTO)
The United Nations
Office at Geneva (UNOG)
The World Intellectual
Property Organization Arbitration and Mediation Center (WIPO)
The Court of
Arbitration for Sport (CAS)

This unique setting allows students to learn not only from books, but
from the heartbeat of international law itself.

Scholarships & Support - Scholarships that open doors

for students financing their studies independently.

The MIDS is taught by some of the most respected voices in international dispute settlement, including:

  • Professor Zachary Douglas KC (external link) - Leading authority on investment arbitration
  • Professor Gabrielle Kaufmann-Kohler (external link) - Former President of the Swiss Arbitration Association
  • Alexander Fessas (external link) - Secretary General of the ICC International Court of Arbitration

Students benefit from direct engagement with faculty who shape global legal practice - through lectures, tutorials, and seminars.   Located in Geneva, the MIDS offers unmatched proximity to institutions like:

  • The World Trade Organization (WTO)
  • The United Nations Office at Geneva (UNOG)
  • The World Intellectual Property Organization Arbitration and Mediation Center (WIPO)
  • The Court of Arbitration for Sport (CAS)

This unique setting allows students to learn not only from books, but from the heartbeat of international law itself.
