Tuition fees for the 2024-2025 MIDS program are set at CHF 28’000. 

As per MIDS regulation, tuitions fees are due and non-refundable once the academic year has started.

The MIDS reserves the right to modify, postpone or cancel the Program upon unforeseen circumstances, in particular if the face-to-face conditions and the use of the facilities cannot be maintained; we reserve the right to transform the on-site teaching to a live virtual classroom.

According to international rankings, Geneva figures among the cities with the highest quality of life... and among the most expensive ones. For students, this has a direct impact on living expenses which must be taken into account when planning your budget for the year. MIDS estimates that a minimum of CHF 1'500 - 2'000 per month is needed to cover living expenses during the program.

To confirm the enrolment in the MIDS program, admitted students must pay a non-refundable deposit of CHF 700 within three weeks after the notification of their admission. This amount will be credited towards the tuition fees.

The remaining tuition fees are payable in three instalments according to the following schedule:

  • CHF 3’300 by 20 May 2024
  • CHF 12’000 by 15 July 2024
  • CHF 12’000 by 1 October 2024
