Congratulations on your admission to the MIDS LL.M. in International Dispute Settlement!
We are delighted to welcome you to the 2026-2027 cohort of this joint
programme of the University of Geneva Faculty of Law and the Graduate
Institute of International and Development Studies. Your selection
reflects the Admissions Committee's strong appreciation of your academic
excellence, professional promise, and motivation for advanced study in
international dispute settlement. As an admitted student, you are invited to confirm your place in the 2026-2027 cohort by submitting the signed Acceptance Form and the CHF 700 deposit by 13 March 2026 . You will find below the Tuition Fee Payment Information and the Acceptance Form, which should be downloaded, completed, signed, and returned to info@mids.ch Candidates
wishing to be considered for a MIDS‑managed scholarship may also
complete the Scholarship Expression of Interest form by 12 February 2026 . Financial supporting documents related to the information provided in this EoI will be requested from shortlisted candidates. Scholarship results will be communicated in mid-March.

We look forward to
guiding you through the next steps of enrollment and to welcoming you in
Geneva for an enriching year of study, dialogue, and professional
growth.

We are delighted to welcome you to the 2026-2027 cohort of this joint programme of the University of Geneva Faculty of Law and the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies. Your selection reflects the Admissions Committee's strong appreciation of your academic excellence, professional promise, and motivation for advanced study in international dispute settlement.

As an admitted student, you are invited to confirm your place in the 2026-2027 cohort by submitting the signed Acceptance Form and the CHF 700 deposit by 13 March 2026. You will find below the Tuition Fee Payment Information and the Acceptance Form, which should be downloaded, completed, signed, and returned to info@mids.ch

Candidates wishing to be considered for a MIDS‑managed scholarship (external link) may also complete the Scholarship Expression of Interest (external link) form by 12 February 2026. Financial supporting documents related to the information provided in this EoI will be requested from shortlisted candidates. Scholarship results will be communicated in mid-March.

We look forward to guiding you through the next steps of enrollment and to welcoming you in Geneva for an enriching year of study, dialogue, and professional growth.
