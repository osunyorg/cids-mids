Congratulations on your conditional admission to the MIDS LL.M. in International Dispute Settlement!
We are delighted to welcome you to the 2026-2027 cohort of this joint
programme of the University of Geneva Faculty of Law and the Graduate
Institute of International and Development Studies. Your selection
reflects the Admissions Committee's strong appreciation of your academic
excellence, professional promise, and motivation for advanced study in
international dispute settlement. As a conditionally admitted student, you are invited to confirm your place in the 2026-2027 cohort by submitting the signed Acceptance Form and the CHF 700 deposit by the deadline communicated to you in your admission communications.

You will find below the Tuition Fee Payment Information and the Acceptance Form, which should be downloaded, completed, signed, and returned to info@mids.ch Please note that, in line with the programme's admissions policy,
candidates admitted from the priority reserve (waiting) list are not
eligible for consideration for MIDS-managed scholarships.  We look forward to
guiding you through the next steps of enrollment and to welcoming you in
Geneva for an enriching year of study, dialogue, and professional
growth.
