CIDS Council
The CIDS Council is the overarching body supervising both the Center for International Dispute Settlement (CIDS) and the MIDS. Half of its members are appointed by the University of Geneva, the other half by the Geneva Graduate Institute. The CIDS Council sets the strategy and oversees all matters regarding the functioning of the Center.
