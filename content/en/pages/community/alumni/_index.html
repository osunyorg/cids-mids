notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- Staying involved as an alumn slug: >- staying-involved-as-an-alumn ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: features title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false children: 3 data: description: >-

Whether you feel nostalgic of your time at the MIDS or feel the need to give back or reconnect after a years of legal practice, there are many ways for you to stay involved with the MIDS after you have graduated.

elements: - title: >- Share job opportunities with us description: >-

We always value the contribution of alumni in letting us know about job opportunities in their law firms or organizations.

alt: >- credit: >- - title: >- Hire MIDS students description: >-

You are hiring and would like a MIDS student or recent graduate to join your team? Write to us at info@mids.ch and we will do our best to find a student or recent graduate who matches your needs.

alt: >- credit: >- - title: >- Mentor new MIDS students description: >-

You can give back and reconnect with the MIDS by volunteering to mentor new MIDS students. This is a very flexible and low-key way for you to stay involved and connect with your successors at the MIDS.

alt: >- credit: >- - title: >- Help us recruit future MIDS students description: >-

Because we believe that nobody is in a better position to know what students need to succeed in the MIDS than former students themselves, we seek the input of alumni in our admissions process. If you are interested, please email us at info@mids.ch.

alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- MIDS Alumni Association slug: >- mids-alumni-association ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: chapter title: >- About the MIDS Alumni Association slug: >- about-the-mids-alumni-association ranks: self: 3 data: layout: no_background text: >-

The MIDS Alumni Association ("MAA") was founded in 2022 for the purposes of maintaining connections between alumni, helping alumni to integrate the work market, promoting their work, contributing to the field of international dispute settlement and favoring access to MIDS studies. It is a non-profit association incorporated under the laws of Geneva, Switzerland.

View the association's statutes (PDF).

The MAA is open to all Alumni students of MIDS. Are you an alumnus? Register here - external link.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- Activities slug: >- activities ranks: self: 3 data: layout: no_background text: >-

In line with its statutes, the MAA is engaged in the following activities:

  • Maintaining the database of MIDS Alumni and helping build bridges and connections between them
  • Organizing events related to international dispute settlement
  • Promoting the work, achievements and contributions of the Alumni to the field of international dispute settlement
  • Supporting Alumni entry into job market
  • Operating and maintaining local or regional desks
  • Contributing to the MIDS Alumni Scholarship to help new students attend the MIDS
