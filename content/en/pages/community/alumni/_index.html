The MIDS offers a truly international program with faculty members coming from all over the world and affiliated to prestigious universities. This diversity is also reflected in its Alumni.

Since 2008, the MIDS has trained 397 students representing 85 nationalities.

MIDS Alumni generally go on to careers in the foreign affairs, trade or justice departments of governments, in arbitration institutions, international organizations, NGOs or in international law departments of law firms or companies.

MIDS Alumni keep close tights with the program as it was illustrated by the over 200 of them who came back to Geneva to celebrate the 10th Anniversary of the MIDS, on 28-29 September 2018.
