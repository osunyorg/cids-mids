Completed in 2012 a footbridge away from the Maison de la Paix, the Edgar and Danièle de Picciotto Student Residence is a five-minute walk from parc Mon Repos, the lakeside and the United Nations headquarters. The number 15 tram stops directly outside the residence, providing quick and easy connections to the city centre and Cornavin train station.

The Edgar and Danièle de Picciotto Student Residence is also walking distance from the Pâquis, an international and lively neighborhood with many restaurants and grocery shops.

Ideal if you are looking for accommodation in a smaller-scale environment. Located in an urban setting conveniently located next to the Institute, the Edgar and Danièle de Picciotto Student Residence includes:

  • one common room (tables and chairs, billiards table, microwave)
  • a fitness centre
  • laundry facilities

Built in 2021 and situated on a hillside, the Grand Morillon Student Residence is located near the International Labour Organization and United Nations Geneva headquarters. In addition, it is only a five minute bus ride or a 20-minute walk from the Maison de la paix . The residence features a variety of common spaces, including three rooftops, one of which offers magnificent views of Lake Geneva, Mont Blanc and Jura mountains.

Ideal if you are looking for an international campus-life, the Student Residence includes:

  • a grocery store
  • a restaurant
  • a coffee shop
  • indoor sport facility 
  • meeting and study rooms
  • a recreation room
  • a fitness centre and a multifunctional hall
  • community gardens, rooftops, greenspaces
  • laundry facilities
