author: >- Aparna Nagabhushana, MIDS'23 job: >- Associate, Trilegal (India) photo: id: "0fde456e-8a39-4eaa-84d8-35c1ed15e63c" file: "0fde456e-8a39-4eaa-84d8-35c1ed15e63c" - kind: block template: title title: >- What is it like to study at the MIDS? slug: >- what-is-it-like-to-study-at-the-mids ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: 2 data: layout: no_background text: >-

An engaging and dynamic learning environment, driven by experienced faculty who bring real-world insights into the classroom. Students benefit from a close-knit and diverse community that fosters collaboration and an exchange of ideas. With small class sizes, students enjoy close interactions with faculty, receiving personalized guidance and mentorship.

The varied pedagogy, comprising lectures, seminars, case studies and practical exercises, caters to different learning styles and promotes active participation. Our rigorous and intensive program values critical thinking and debate. We prepare students for the complexities of international dispute resolution through a comprehensive and immersive academic journey.

Being a good lawyer requires more than knowledge, skills and experience: it requires being a well-rounded, open-minded, and collaborative individual. As such, for all the rigor, demands, and healthy dose of competition involved at the MIDS, it is also meant to be a collaborative journey. Through group projects, discussions, and networking opportunities, students develop essential teamwork skills, crucial for their future careers, and build lasting connections.
