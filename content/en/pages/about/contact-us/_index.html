Please note that the MIDS follows the Geneva Graduate Institute’s staff holiday schedule. As a result, the MIDS Office is closed on the following dates: 

  • Friday, 20 December 2024 to Monday, 6 January 2025 (Winter closure)
  • Friday, 18 April and Monday, 21 April 2025 (Easter Friday/Monday)
  • Thursday, 1 May 2025 (Labor Day)
  • Thursday, 29 May 2025 (Ascension)
  • Monday, 9 June 2025 (Pentecost or Whit Monday)
  • Friday, 1 August 2025 (Swiss National Day)
  • Thursday, 11 September 2025 (Jeûne Genevois)
