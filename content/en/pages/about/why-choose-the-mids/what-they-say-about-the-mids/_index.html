On the occasion of the 15th anniversary of the MIDS, we asked our alumni to tell us what they thought of the MIDS.

"The MIDS was academically the most challenging thing I had done. I had to spend a lot of time reading and catching up with legal concepts that were unknown to me at that point. Nevertheless, it is still one of the most rewarding experiences I have had. I would absolutely do it all over again!"

Great Rios, MIDS'09

"TheÂ MIDS was a wonderful experience, allowing me to immerse myself for a year in a stimulating environment where I was able to deepen my knowledge of international law and international trade law. ... I have also learned to look at problems from different angles."

Pietro Acerbi, MIDS'10

"The MIDS was an enriching and life/career-changing experience.... I was particularly pleased with the exposure to high-caliber arbitration practitioners and the multicultural environment of the program. The intra-MIDS moot court was perhaps my highlight of the program. Putting legal knowledge to test under a hearing-like environment was no easy task, but in my view it summed up the work of an arbitration practitioner and strengthened the ties among students as well."

Yuri Mantilla, MIDS'12

"The MIDS not only paved the way to my current job in international arbitration in Switzerland; it also gave me access to a huge network of international disputes lawyers, many of whom I now call friends. It was a career and life-changing experience."

Courtney Furner, MIDS'17

"My time at the MIDS was both intense, delightful, fun, and eye-opening"

Sashia Diandra Anindita, MIDS'23

"What I experienced [at the MIDS] was so much more than an academic program. I truly grew as an individual."

Sanskriti Sharma, MIDS'23
