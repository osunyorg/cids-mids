By recruiting students and alumni from the MIDS you are incorporating some of the finest international dispute settlement lawyers. With its over 12 years of experience, the MIDS has an established reputation of providing talented lawyers worldwide to law firms, international organizations, governments, companies, arbitral institutions and international tribunals. 80% of our students are placed before they finish their studies and the remaining ones have all found a job within 6 months after graduation.

We are often approached by numerous employers seeking a particular profile. Our graduates and alumni are an international pool of lawyers with knowledge of very diverse jurisdictions, languages, and substantive legal backgrounds, and a solid international procedural law expertise.

Because we are well aware of the constraints and the needs of employers in the field of international dispute settlement, we offer a range of services to help you in your recruitment process: 

  • Conduct interviews on campus: we are pleased to help arrange the space and time for you to conduct interviews on campus with a short-listed group of candidates of your interest.
  • Organize a corporate talk for the students to attend: we welcome corporate presentations to our students where they can get to know better your work.
  • Join our academic retreat as an arbitrator: the MIDS academic retreat consists of an internal moot hearing exercise where students plead before an arbitral tribunal composed of real practitioners. Join us for the activity and get to see our students in practice.
  • Post job vacancies through the MIDS career services: we circulate to our network of students and alumni offers from the employers with whom we cooperate on a regular basis.
  • Meet the career services team to discuss your specific recruitment needs: our team is fully committed to provide assistance in identifying potential recruits for you.
