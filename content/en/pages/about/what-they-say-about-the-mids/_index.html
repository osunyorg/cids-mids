The impact of MIDS is best captured by those who've lived it. Here's what alumni have to say:

body: >-


"The MIDS was a wonderful experience, allowing me to immerse myself for a year in a stimulating environment where I was able to deepen my knowledge of international law and international trade law. ... I have also learned to look at problems from different angles." Pietro Acerbi, MIDS'10 "The MIDS was academically the most challenging thing I had done. I
had to spend a lot of time reading and catching up with legal concepts that
were unknown to me at that point. Nevertheless, it is still one of the most
rewarding experiences I have had. I would absolutely do it all over again!" Great Rios, MIDS'09 "My time at the MIDS was both intense, delightful, fun, and eye-opening" Sashia Diandra Anindita, MIDS'23

"I chose the MIDS because it
is recognized as Europe's leading program in arbitration and dispute
resolution. And it proved worthy of its reputation: intense, demanding, and
absolutely transformative. Giovanna Martins Santana, MIDS'25


"The MIDS was an enriching and life/career-changing experience.... I was particularly pleased with the exposure to high-caliber arbitration practitioners and the multicultural environment of the program. The intra-MIDS moot court was perhaps my highlight of the program. Putting legal knowledge to test under a hearing-like environment was no easy task, but in my view it summed up the work of an arbitration practitioner and strengthened the ties among students as well." Yuri Mantilla, MIDS'12 "The MIDS not only paved the way to my current job in international arbitration in Switzerland; it also gave me access to a huge network of international disputes lawyers, many of whom I now call friends. It was a career and life-changing experience." Courtney Furner, MIDS'17 "What I experienced [at the MIDS] was so much more than an academic program. I truly grew as an individual." Sanskriti Sharma, MIDS'23 "This past year has been an
incredible journey. I had the opportunity to deepen my understanding of
international dispute settlement while connecting with inspiring people from
all over the world."
Ana Luisa Sousa, MIDS'25

"I applied to the MIDS hoping it would help me start a career in
international dispute settlement. The reality far exceeded my expectations; it
changed my life. I recommend it without reservation." Ndanga Kamau, MIDS'10 "The MIDS was an extremely valuable addition to my CV. It's a
degree that carries a lot of weight with law firms and
practitioners." 

Vamika Puri, MIDS'21 "After one year of intense
classroom discussions, attending arbitration conferences, study trips and
lectures from leading practitioners... I can attest to the fact that it has
been one of the biggest privileges of my life to be part of this incredible
program."
Shreyansh Jain, MIDS'25

 

"I not only gained invaluable knowledge and laid the basis for a
professional network at the MIDS: it also opened doors to a truly international
career and changed my life."

Anna Kozmenko, MIDS'09 "The small cohort ensured that interactions with instructors and
fellow students were always stimulating and enriching."
Aparna Nagabhushana, MIDS'23


"The past year has been the most challenging and rewarding journey of my
life, filled with humbling academic challenges, moments of self-doubt and
countless discussions that pushed me to grow. Every step was worth it, and I am
proud to now be part of the MIDS Alumni community. I am deeply grateful to my professors and classmates, who made this experience
not only intellectually enriching but also unforgettable on a personal
level." Nitsa Siradze, MIDS'25
