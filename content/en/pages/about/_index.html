Short for "Masters in International Dispute Settlement", the MIDS is an accredited top-tier LL.M. program based in Geneva, Switzerland.

Offered jointly by the University of Geneva Faculty of Law - external link and the Geneva Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies - external link, it delivers advanced legal training in international dispute settlement.
