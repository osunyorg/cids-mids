Welcome to the MIDS!
As you prepare to join the programme, please consult the Welcome Guide, which provides key information on administrative procedures, arrival planning, and student life in Geneva.  It covers visa and residence permit requirements, accommodation, health insurance, and practical aspects of settling in, and should be used to prepare ahead of the start of the academic year.
Please review the guide carefully and complete the relevant steps in a timely manner.  This page will be updated regularly. Please consult it periodically for the latest information.  We look forward to your arrival in Geneva!


Welcome Guide 2026–2027

Accommodation

Students are responsible for securing accommodation in Geneva. Given the
high demand, arrangements should be made as early as possible.  All
admitted students are invited to complete the Accommodation Application
Form, shared via email, to indicate their housing preferences and
interest in the available accommodation options.  Two main accommodation options are available:


IHEID student residences (Grand Morillon and Edgar & Danièle de Picciotto)   Applications must be submitted directly by you through the Graduate Institute Housing portal .


RUI residences (Rothschild, Carlton, La Voie Creuse)   Admitted
students interested in these options must indicate this through the
Accommodation  Application Form. Those who express interest will be
contacted directly by RUI with further instructions.



Please note that accommodation is subject to availability.
Rooms are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis, and
preferences cannot be guaranteed.

Students are responsible for securing accommodation in Geneva. Given the high demand, arrangements should be made as early as possible. 

All admitted students are invited to complete the Accommodation Application Form, shared via email, to indicate their housing preferences and interest in the available accommodation options. 

Two main accommodation options are available:

  • IHEID student residences (Grand Morillon and Edgar & Danièle de Picciotto) 
    Applications must be submitted directly by you through the Graduate Institute Housing portal (external link).
  • RUI residences (Rothschild, Carlton, La Voie Creuse) 
    Admitted students interested in these options must indicate this through the Accommodation  Application Form. Those who express interest will be contacted directly by RUI with further instructions.

Please note that accommodation is subject to availability. Rooms are allocated on a first-come, first-served basis, and preferences cannot be guaranteed.
