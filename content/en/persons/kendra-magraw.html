Kendra Magraw specializes in international dispute settlement (particularly State-to-State, investment, commercial and sports arbitration), as well as issues related to public international law and international economic law. She has worked in varying capacities in private practice, international organizations and dispute settlement bodies. She has authored, co-authored, edited and contributed to numerous publications related to public international law and international dispute settlement.
