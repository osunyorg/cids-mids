Alexander is a Bolivian-qualified lawyer with over four years of experience in civil and commercial litigation, as well as in domestic and international commercial arbitration. He graduated with distinction (Magna Cum Laude) from the Universidad Privada Boliviana (UPB), with a specialization in International Law.

As an associate at Wayar & von Borries S.C., a leading Bolivian firm specializing in dispute resolution, he has gained extensive experience litigating contract disputes, particularly in infrastructure, construction, energy, and natural resources projects, as well as in white-collar crime cases. He has also acted as counsel in arbitration proceedings under the rules of the ICC, the Chamber of Industry, Commerce, Services, and Tourism of Santa Cruz (CAINCO), and the National Chamber of Commerce of Bolivia (CNC).
