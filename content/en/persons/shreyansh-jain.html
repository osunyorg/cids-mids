Shreyansh is an India-qualified lawyer with an expertise in International Commercial Arbitration and Sports Arbitration. He has acted in Domestic as well as International arbitrations across ad-hoc and institutional (LCIA and SIAC). He has also served as a Tribunal Secretary to Mr. Vyapak Desai, FCIArb in an International Commercial Arbitration seated in Kuala Lumpur. Further, he has also represented various international athletes in sports disputes before the Delhi High Court as well as before institutions such as National Anti-Doping Agency (NADA) and the Sports Arbitration Centre of India (SACI).

Shreyansh started his independent arbitration practice (SJ-Arb Offices) specializing in all forms of disputes pertaining to Commercial and Sports arbitration. He also served as a drafting member of the Sports Arbitration Council of India (SACI) and the Mumbai Centre for International Arbitration (MCIA). Prior to establishing his independent practice, Shreyansh worked with the International Disputes Team of Nishith Desai Associates (NDA), a top-tier International law firm based out of India for over a year. NDA has been the proud recipient of the Financial Times – RSG award 4 times in a row, (2014-2017) as the Most Innovative Indian Law Firm, and has its offices based out of Mumbai, Palo Alto (Silicon Valley), Bangalore, Singapore, New Delhi, Munich, and New York. Apart from that, Shreyansh has been affiliated to the chambers of various Senior Advocates practicing in International Commercial Arbitrations in Singapore and England.

He completed his BA L.L.B (Hons.) at O.P Jindal Global University in 2020. He represented his university in international moot court as well as ADR competitions. He stood runners-up in the ELSA Maynooth Negotiation Competition in 2018. 

Besides his legal background, Shreyansh is a trained classical singer and has also attained a double distinction in Rabindra Sangeet.
