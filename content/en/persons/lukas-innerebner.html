Lukas specializes in the resolution of commercial, contractual and corporate disputes, with extensive experience in arbitration. As former Legal Counsel at the Swiss Arbitration Centre, he has administered more than 150 domestic and international arbitrations - an expertise that often makes the difference in arbitration proceedings.

As Swiss-qualified lawyer, he also regularly represents companies and individuals before state courts and advises clients on contract drafting.

In the past, he has advised companies, banks, and investors on M&A transactions and capital markets law, gaining in-depth knowledge for post-M&A and corporate disputes.

Lukas is an Italian national and also advises clients in Italian.
