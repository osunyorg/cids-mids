Mr. Senogles has a broad accounting practice; while specialising in international arbitration.

Having been a VP and a Partner in American and Canadian firms respectively, he has recently opened an independent finance practice in Switzerland, in which to take on a broad range of work. As a chartered accountant with an ICAEW practising certificate and 30 years' experience, together with his partners, Senogles & Co is designed to deliver cost-effective advice and action.

With a caseload principally focused on commercial arbitration mandates, Mr. Senogles is engaged in the following areas:

  • Training and teaching - on finance, accounting, damages and the role of the expert witness
  • Sports clubs and federations - club finance management, FFP, disputes at TAS/CAS
  • War reparations - advising governments
  • Joint Ventures - JV entity accounting reviews (ad-hoc or regular)
  • Corporate advisory - non-executive / advisory board seats, governance
  • Private clients - private banking reviews, financial management, real estate sales

He is Welsh chartered accountant; based near Geneva since 2000 when he began working on staff at the United Nations Compensation Commission. He has taught the "Financial Damages" workshops at MIDS since 2010 and is also a member of the teaching team at CIDS.
