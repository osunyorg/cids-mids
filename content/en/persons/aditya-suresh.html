I am presently qualifying as a solicitor in England & Wales, and have taken SQE-1 in January 2026.

I have over two years' professional experience in international commercial and investment arbitration. As a trainee at Freshfields and Quinn Emanuel's Paris offices, I assisted with preparing submissions in some of the world's most challenging international commercial and investment arbitrations involving international corporate entities, States, State entities, and international organisations.Â
