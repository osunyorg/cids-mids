Atie Babaie is a legal professional specializing in contract management and dispute resolution, with a focus on energy, oil and gas, environmental, transportation, ESG, and construction sectors. She is dedicated to advancing sustainable business practices through the strategic use of legal frameworks and policy development.

Holding an LL.M. in International Dispute Settlement from Geneva and currently pursuing a doctorate in law in Switzerland, Atie combines strong academic foundations with extensive practical experience. With proficiency in English and German and over eight years of international commercial practice, she supports the growth and expansion of business initiatives across Europe and the Middle East. Through innovative legal and business strategies, she navigates diverse regulatory environments to deliver pragmatic, results-driven solutions in complex markets.
