Zacarias is an India-qualified dispute resolution lawyer with a little over 9 (nine) years' experience representing clients before various Indian courts and arbitral tribunals. Prior to the MIDS he worked as a Principal Associate in the Dispute Resolution practice group at Khaitan & Co, Mumbai. 

Zacarias specializes in representing multinational corporations in complex international commercial arbitrations, including cases administered by the International Chamber of Commerce, Singapore International Arbitration Centre and the Indian Merchants Chamber. His expertise spans across various sectors, from patent disputes and joint-venture disagreements to family succession disputes and insolvency matters. 

He regularly contributes to legal writing and has published for the NLS Business Law Review Blog, Lexology and the Indian Corporate Law Blog.

Zacarias holds a Bachelor of Laws from Savitribai Phule Pune University, Pune, India.
