Chizaram is a Senior Associate in the Dispute Resolution & Risk Management practice at Aluko & Oyebode, Lagos, Nigeria.

She advises and represents clients in commercial litigation proceedings as well as institutional arbitrations under the ICSID, ICC, and LCIA Arbitration Rules. Her experience cuts across disputes arising out of several sectors and industries including energy, construction, mining, technology, and finance.

Chizaram holds a Bachelor of Laws from the University of Nigeria, and a Master’s in International Dispute Settlement from the University of Geneva and the Graduate Institute of International and Development Studies (Geneva MIDS).

She is the founder Arbitration Podium, an initiative that highlights arbitration developments across the continent. She also currently serves as Co-Director of Scholarships at Young ICCA, and as Ambassador for Swiss Arbitration in Nigeria, under the Swiss Arbitration Association (ASA) Ambassador Programme.
