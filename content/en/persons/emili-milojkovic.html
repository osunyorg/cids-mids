Emili joined the MIDS team in April 2025 as an Administrative Assistant, where she is responsible for the day-to-day administration and logistics required for the delivery of the MIDS Program. Prior to joining the MIDS, Emili worked for 14 years in the automotive, medical and cosmetic industries specialising in administrative coordination, procurement, and project management.

Emili holds a Master's degree in International Trade.
