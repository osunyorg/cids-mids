Adrián Martínez Benoit is a seasoned legal professional with over a decade of experience in international organizations, specializing in humanitarian operations, technology, and dispute resolution. Currently serving as Senior Legal Officer (Legal Business Manager) at the International Organization for Migration (IOM) in Geneva, Adrián leads the development and implementation of knowledge management tools, business processes, and change management initiatives within the Office of Legal Affairs. Previously, Adrian served as Regional Legal Adviser for the Americas at the International Federation of Red Cross and Red Crescent Societies (IFRC), where he was responsible for negotiating privileges and immunities agreements with states, supporting disaster response operations and advising on partnership agreements with public and private donors. He is a native Spanish speaker, fluent in English and has an intermediate proficiency in French and Italian.
