Why Geneva? 

  • A birthplace of modern international law and a city that has made history
  • A hub for expertise and diplomacy, with hundreds of international organizations, NGOs, and law firms
  • A top destination for arbitration, in the top 5 worldwide alongside London, Singapore, Paris, and Hong Kong

All of it in a city on a human scale, easy to live in and at the heart of Europe.
