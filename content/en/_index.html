Geneva is a city where big ideas meet expertise.

A birthplace of modern international law, a hub of expertise and diplomacy with hundreds of international organizations, NGOs, and law firms, and top 5 worldwide preferred seat of arbitration, alongside London, Singapore, Hong Kong, and Paris, Geneva is an unparalleled location to study international dispute settlement.
