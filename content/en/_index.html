A strategic location

A hub for expertise and diplomacy
Geneva is home to hundreds of international organizations, NGOs, corporations, and law firms.

A top destination for arbitration
The Swiss city is in the top 5 worldwide alongside London, Singapore, Paris, and Hong Kong.

A unique place to live
Geneva is also an exceptionally livable, picturesque, and cosmopolitan city, at the heart of Europe.

A one-of-a-kind program

Not an ordinary LL.M.
With its niche focus, unique curriculum, and solid reputation, the MIDS is an unusual rival to the world's most prestigious LL.M. programs.

Practice-oriented
Everything at the MIDS is designed to prepare students for a successful career in one of the most competitive fields of the legal industry.

An unparalleled community
In less than two decades, the MIDS has constituted one of the world's largest communities of international dispute settlement lawyers.
