At the heart of Europe, Geneva has been at the center of international relations for more than a century. It is where major international legal instruments were born and where the League of Nations laid the foundations for modern international cooperation. Then and now, Geneva is a place where big ideas meet expertise to try and build a more peaceful and prosperous world.
