Maison de la Paix, Auditorium A1-B. Registration needed.
Do not miss the day of discussions celebrating our alumni community, organized by the MIDS Alumni Association (MAA).

body: >-


This year's conference will revolve around four main topics: - "We're losing the ISDS fight"? If so, what next? - Navigating Corruption-related Claims in Commercial Arbitration - Battle of the Landmark Decisions - Combating Climate Change through International Law: Symbolism or Effective Change?  Who is this event for? All students, professors, as well as seasoned arbitration practitioners are encouraged to join us in these interesting discussions, either in-person or online. Attendance is free of charge but registration is required  here .  Voluntary contribution to the MIDS Alumni Scholarship If you would like to help prospective students access the MIDS education, you can click this  link  to make a donation. Location Auditorium A1-B Maison de la Paix - Graduate Institute Chemin Eugène-Rigot 2 1202 Geneva

This year's conference will revolve around four main topics:

- "We're losing the ISDS fight"? If so, what next?

- Navigating Corruption-related Claims in Commercial Arbitration

- Battle of the Landmark Decisions

- Combating Climate Change through International Law: Symbolism or Effective Change? 

Who is this event for?

All students, professors, as well as seasoned arbitration practitioners are encouraged to join us in these interesting discussions, either in-person or online. Attendance is free of charge but registration is required here. 

Voluntary contribution to the MIDS Alumni Scholarship

If you would like to help prospective students access the MIDS education, you can click this link to make a donation.
