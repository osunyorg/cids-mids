Do not miss the day of discussions celebrating our Alumni Community, organized by the MIDS Alumni Association (MAA).

Location: Maison de la Paix, Auditorium A1-B.

Registration required here (external link).

body: >-


This year's conference will revolve around four main topics:

- Sanctions-related Disputes in ISDS: Justiciability & Legitimacy - Navigating Sovereign Immunity in Enforcement Proceedings - Battle of the Landmark Decisions - Re-centering the Human in International Arbitration
Who is this event for? All students, professors, as well as
seasoned arbitration practitioners are encouraged to join us in these
interesting discussions. Attendance is free
of charge but registration is required  here . The event is only available in person.
Location Auditorium A1-B Maison de la Paix - Graduate Institute Chemin Eugène-Rigot 2 1202 Geneva
