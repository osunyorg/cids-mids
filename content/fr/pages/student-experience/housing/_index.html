Completed in 2012 a footbridge away from the Maison de la Paix, the Edgar and Danièle de Picciotto Student Residence is a five-minute walk from parc Mon Repos, the lakeside and the United Nations headquarters. The number 15 tram stops directly outside the residence, providing quick and easy connections to the city centre and Cornavin train station.

The Edgar and Danièle de Picciotto Student Residence is also walking distance from the Pâquis, an international and lively neighborhood with many restaurants and grocery shops.

Ideal if you are looking for accommodation in a smaller-scale environment. Located in an urban setting conveniently located next to the Institute, the Edgar and Danièle de Picciotto Student Residence includes:

  • one common room (tables and chairs, billiards table, microwave)
  • a fitness centre
  • laundry facilities

Built in 2021 and situated on a hillside, the Grand Morillon Student Residence is located near the International Labour Organization and United Nations Geneva headquarters. In addition, it is only a five minute bus ride or a 20-minute walk from the Maison de la paix . The residence features a variety of common spaces, including three rooftops, one of which offers magnificent views of Lake Geneva, Mont Blanc and Jura mountains.

Ideal if you are looking for an international campus-life, the Student Residence includes:

  • a grocery store
  • a restaurant
  • a coffee shop
  • indoor sport facility 
  • meeting and study rooms
  • a recreation room
  • a fitness centre and a multifunctional hall
  • community gardens, rooftops, greenspaces
  • laundry facilities

First opened in 1974, the Résidence Universitaire Internationale (RUI) Rothschild in Geneva is a well-equipped student housing facility located in the heart of the city. It offers 113 furnished accommodations, including both rooms and studios. Each unit comes with a shared or private kitchenette and bathroom. The residence provides several essential services to ensure a comfortable stay for students, such as weekly cleaning, bi-weekly linen changes, and free Wi-Fi (though not guaranteed). Additionally, the residence features a cafeteria, a conference room, and a smaller meeting room, making it a conducive environment for both living and studying. The RUI Rothschild prioritizes international students, fostering a diverse and inclusive community.

One of the most cost-effective options available to MIDS students, it offers single rooms for as little as 650 CHF/month, or studios for 800 CHF/month.

The primary and largest student residence managed by the University of Geneva, the Cité Universitaire de Genève is a vibrant student residence located in the Champel district of Geneva. It comprises four buildings that offer a variety of accommodations, including individual rooms, studios for two people, and family apartments. The residence is designed to foster a supportive and communal living environment, with amenities such as two restaurants, a grocery store, and a laundry facility. Additionally, the Cité Universitaire provides various spaces for meetings, events, and recreational activities, including a sauna and sports halls³. This residence is open to students aged 18 to 35 who are enrolled at the University of Geneva or other higher education institutions³. It also welcomes short-term guests during university holidays³, making it a versatile and inclusive option for both local and international students.
