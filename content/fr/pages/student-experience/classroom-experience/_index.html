The classroom experience at the MIDS is characterized by an engaging and dynamic learning environment, driven by experienced faculty who bring real-world insights into the classroom. Students benefit from a close-knit, diverse community that fosters collaboration and the exchange of ideas. With small class sizes, students enjoy close interactions with faculty, receiving personalized guidance and mentorship. The varied pedagogy, which includes lectures, seminars, case studies, and practical exercises, caters to different learning styles and promotes active participation. This rigorous and intensive program emphasizes critical thinking and debate, preparing students for the complexities of international dispute resolution through a comprehensive and immersive academic journey.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- Varied pedagogy slug: >- varied-pedagogy ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: image title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: text: >- image: id: "0f794735-927a-471e-87fe-7db2837c7f8f" file: "0f794735-927a-471e-87fe-7db2837c7f8f" alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Because of the diversity of subjects and the variety in the background and approach of our faculty, the curriculum employs a mix of lectures, seminars, case studies, and practical exercises, ensuring that students are exposed to a broad range of teaching methods that cater to different learning styles. During their time at the MIDS, all students will therefore be exposed to both familiar and novel methodologies.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- Cultivating debates and critical thinking slug: >- cultivating-debates-and-critical-thinking ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: image title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: text: >- image: id: "d98ce1fa-eb0c-4a7f-9f68-0aa19c972f6a" file: "d98ce1fa-eb0c-4a7f-9f68-0aa19c972f6a" alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

The classroom environment, in addition to the range of profiles and experiences of students and faculty, encourage robust debates and critical analysis, helping students to develop their argumentative skills, gain exposure to different perspectives, and ultimately deepen their understanding of international dispute settlement.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- Practice-oriented active learning slug: >- practice-oriented-active-learning ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: image title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: text: >- image: id: "dca16dfd-dad1-4d37-9093-1dd68cced83c" file: "dca16dfd-dad1-4d37-9093-1dd68cced83c" alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Because our goal is to provide our graduates with practical knowledge and actionable skills that will help them thrive in their career, active participation is a cornerstone of the MIDS experience, with students regularly engaging in discussions, simulations, and group projects that promote hands-on learning and collaboration.

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- A collaborative journey slug: >- a-collaborative-journey ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Being a good lawyer requires more than knowledge, skills and experience: it requires being a well-rounded, sensitive, open-minded, empathetic and collaborative individual. As such, for all the rigor, demands, and healthy dose of competition involved in the MIDS program, it is also meant to be a collaborative journey enabling students to develop their soft-skills and thrive together rather than against each other. This cooperative approach cultivates a supportive learning environment, encouraging the exchange of diverse perspectives and ideas, and fostering mutual growth. Through group projects, discussions, and networking opportunities, students develop essential teamwork skills, crucial for their future careers, and build lasting professional and personal relationships. 

notes: >- alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: title title: >- An intensive experience slug: >- an-intensive-experience ranks: self: 2 data: layout: classic - kind: block template: image title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: text: >- image: id: "64a43e74-e784-496f-9b92-c1b839e4e3af" file: "64a43e74-e784-496f-9b92-c1b839e4e3af" alt: >- credit: >- - kind: block template: chapter title: >- slug: >- ranks: self: false data: layout: no_background text: >-

Recent graduates often describe the MIDS as a marathon: an intense and sometimes challenging year-long experience, but ultimately an extremely rewarding one. While we do our utmost to make our students' time in Geneva as pleasant and smooth as possible, the program is designed to be immersive, rigorous and demanding, and we encourage prospective students to not underestimate the level of personal commitment it requires. However, with the constant support of our faculty, lecturers, and our experienced and attentive administrative team, this allows students to become the best version of themselves while preparing them for the challenges of international dispute resolution and legal careers.

Furthermore, in the face of the challenges and adversity that are unavoidable in a demanding academic program, our graduates always highlight the extraordinary strength and fulfilling nature of the lifelong bonds they form with each other while at the MIDS.
